What is the ASCOM Initiative?

We are a loosely-knit group of astronomy software developers and astronomy device manufacturers devoted to vendor-independent universal plug-and-play control. ASCOM is a device-neutral language-independent and operating system-independent framework, supported by most astronomy apps and devices on Windows, Linux, and MacOS computers.
