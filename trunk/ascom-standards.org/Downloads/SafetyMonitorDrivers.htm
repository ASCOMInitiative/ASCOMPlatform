Safety Monitor Drivers

Boltwood Safety Monitor (V6.0)
This driver implements Safety Monitor drivers that interface to the Boltwood Weather station and it's clones. Two drivers are provided, OK to Image and OK to Open, they can have different safety limits. This was developed by Chris Rowland. Please address messages relating to this on the ASCOM-Talk Group.
